Planet War

Planet War is a 2D tower defense game. The player can use different monsters to protect the gate to the monsters home. The story is about earth in the year of 2115, it was taken over by monsters, forcing the humans to move out and onto a spaceship. The humans miss their home and feel angery about the monsters, so they decide to take revenge. The player's goal is to use strategy to defend against the humans attack and protect the monster home.
